Breeding

Our Belgian Malinois

We exclusively breed from top Belgian and Dutch Malinois lines. Our primary focus is to breed for the right working temperament. When it comes to raising our puppies we ensure they are fully prepared for life with their new owners, all animals will come with the following:

  • KC Registered
  • 3rd Generation pedigree
  • Fully vacinated
  • Dewormed
  • Crate and litter trained
  • Full health check before leaving us
