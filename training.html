Training

Behavioral training


Our journey started over 10 years ago when we started breeding, training, and selling dogs for the Greek police and for dog sport enthusiasts.
We then slowly started competing ourselves within the I.G.P.
Over the past decade, we have attended countless seminars and presentations from top competitors Including the likes of 6-time world champion Mario Verslype.
After relocating to the UK in 2020 we partnered up with two very reputable companies where we have trained over 300 dogs varying in breed and size covering basic obedience training, aggression, anxiety and full rehabilitation.

Whatever your training needs Tsellios K9 academy has you covered...

Protection / Security training


Ever wanted to make your passion your career? We now offer security canine training.
Thanasis our lead trainer recently delved into the world of protection training and has started his own introduction training programme designed to give you an introductory insight in to the world of security k9 training.
This course is best suited to individuals who are unsure if they want to undertake a professional qualification without any prior experience.
